About Bronze Choco

Bronze Choco was created with a goal and that is to make delicious chocolates that everyone would love. The company was established recently in 2019, and the focus was to be the best Chocolate company in the entire Malaysia and soon the world. The first factory, in the small town of Desa Petaling located in the southern zone of Kuala Lumpur, was set up in 2019.

Bronze Choco started out with manufacturing consumer-packed chocolates that were sold mainly to duty-free shops and supermarkets across the country. In 2020 we relocated our new factory in the Shah Alam Industrial Area where it increased the production lines to provide a diverse range of chocolate products to fulfil the promise to offer high quality chocolates to the customers.

From humble and simple beginnings, Bronze Choco has now become a well-loved consumer brand throughout the country and throughout the entire world. The products have gained the recognition of discerning customers both locally and overseas. Bronze Choco can now be found in over 15 countries around the world including countries like France, Italy and so on.
